# MYMS Positioning

## Core statement

MYMS turns local businesses and independent professionals into digital systems that generate trust, inquiries, bookings and sales.

## Market truth

Most local businesses already have demand channels:

- WhatsApp
- Instagram
- Google
- referrals
- manual sales follow-up

What they usually do not have is structure.

That means:

- no clear landing point
- no reliable booking logic
- no organized CRM
- no measurable follow-up
- no digital trust layer

## Strategic wedge

MYMS does not compete as "another website provider".

It competes as the system that organizes commercial demand around:

- trust
- next-step clarity
- conversion
- operational order

## Product logic

The offer is modular, but not random.

- `Presencia Pro` solves trust and clarity
- `Turnos Pro` solves reservation and schedule friction
- `Ventas Pro` solves lead and seller follow-up
- `Local Pro` solves physical-location conversion

## Messaging principles

- speak like a business operator, not a designer
- focus on lost money, lost leads, lost time and weak trust
- make clear that a page alone is not the value
- show that MYMS is a commercial operating layer

## Anti-positioning

MYMS should not position as:

- a creative agency
- a branding boutique
- a "beautiful websites" service
- a broad enterprise platform with generic SaaS language

## Core message bank

- Tu WhatsApp no es un sistema.
- Instagram muestra. Una landing convierte.
- Menos mensajes. Más turnos.
- Una web no es decoración. Es confianza.
- Cada consulta perdida es plata que no volvió.
- Tu negocio puede reservar mientras dormís.
- Convertí tu negocio en un sistema digital.
